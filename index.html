About Me

I was born in raised in Dallas, Texas, and went to school in Plano. In middle school and high school I played trumpet in the school marching and jazz bands. I also played sports throughout my childhood. Music and sports have a big role in my life; I took part in college intramurals, I've become commissioner of my group of friends' fantasy leage, and have been going to classic rock concerts with my family for as long as I can remember.
